What's EVE Book About

Project was started with an idea of creating social platform for players of EVE Online. A place where players could connect and share their experiences, fan art, images, videos, thoughts, propaganda...

Each "user" would be an actual EVE Online character and you could only login using game's credentials (SSO), this way it would offer a lot of opportunities for role playing characters to work on their characters. Alliances and corporations could share propaganda videos/art and gather followers who could re-share and spread the word.

Whole platform would heavily relay on EVE Online API, so that you could send in game emails, create events (calendar). It would feel as an extension of game itself.

Think of it as combination of r/eve and twitter. A hub of EVE Online community.
